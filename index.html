Rainbow Six Siege is a 5v5 multiplayer tactical shooter. One side's objective is to defend various sites while the other team attacks. Each side is made up of many operators that can be selected, each with a selection of weapons and special tools to give the team an advantage

R6FIX REWARDS UPDATE

Thank you to all players that used R6Fix in the previous year. There are even more rewards up for grabs this year in the form of Alpha Packs. To claim a reward there are several criteria you can meet: Submitting 2 issues that become under investigation, your reported issue reached promoted status, you receive outstanding contribution or you report an exploit.

15/05/2023

Y8S2 Designers Note

There's a variety of balancing work being done for the upcoming update. Operator ban and pick rate are both being used to track popularity of certain operators. An observation blocker gadget is being implemented as another method to deny attackers information surrounding defence tactics that may be in place.

15/05/2023

Anti-Cheat Status Update - May 23

Security behind the scenes has been being improved to make cheat development harder. MMR rollback was disabled temporarily to avoid bugs which have since been fixed which allows MMR rollback to be re-activated. Mousetrap has been launched on consoles to combat input spoofing using external devices.
